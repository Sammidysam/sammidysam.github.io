Welcome! I am Sam Craig. I am Principal Bass of the La Porte County Symphony Orchestra, Assistant Principal Bass of the Fox Valley Orchestra, and a Section Bass in the Lafayette Symphony Orchestra. I teach about 13 bass students spread across the Downers Grove, Illinois area, and the Lafayette, Indiana area. I am a Senior Software Engineer at Resultant. I am now based in Chicago, having grown up in West Lafayette in Indiana and received education at Oberlin College and Conservatory in Ohio.
